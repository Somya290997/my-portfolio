About Me

The combination of hands-on experience and academic learning keeps me sharp and excited about applying emerging technologies to solve real-world challenges. I’m truly passionate about using data to innovate, improve processes, and drive smarter decisions. I’m always eager to collaborate with others who share a similar drive for leveraging data to make a difference.
